My Passions

Beautiful Applications

The best applications, the ones we love to use, are not just bug free. They are gorgous! As developers, we have a responsibility to forge an experience that amazes people.

Performance

One major aspect of user experience is application performance. If an application works perfectly, but is slow users will reject the application. Therfor, its is imporant to maximize performance by being asyncronus and non-blocking.

Scaleability

As an applications user base and data grows, so does the need for it to scale. This, however, is not an easy task. It takes effort at all layers of the tech stack. Every piece of the puzzle tweaked, tuned, and cahced.

Clean Code

I am a big fan of clean code. I believe in things like DRY, reusable code. I love patterns like MVC that helps us structure spaghetti into lasagna.
